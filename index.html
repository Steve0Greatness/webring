How to Join

Email me steve0greatnessiscool+wr [at] gmail.com with a link to your site so that I can make sure you're a good fit for this webring, then I'll let you know your slug. Speaking of which, make sure to include something like the following on your site: 

<table>
    <tr>
        <td>
            <a
                href="sites/:slug:/prev.xhtml"
            >Prev</a>
        </td>
        <td>
            <a
                href="https://steve0greatness.github.io/webring"
            >
                <img
                    src="logo-500.webp"
                    width="100"
                    height="42"
                />
            </a>
        </td>
        <td>
            <a
                href="sites/:slug:/next.xhtml"
            >Next</a>
        </td>
    </tr>
</table>

Just make sure to replace :slug: with your slug.

Also, it doesn't have to be that specifically, that is just an example.

What gets added to the webring?

I don't accept the following types of sites:

  • Non-personal websites. All websites trying to join this webring must be a personal website. Wether your're hosting a blog, your artwork, or whatnot. It just has to be about you, not an organization, group, or a space of communication. Although you are allowed to have something like a guestbook, as long as it's not the focus of the site.
  • No slurs. Strong language, like swears, are allowed, but if you frequently use slurs on your website, I will not let you in the webring.
  • No anti-queer material. If you are against the rights or existance of those who are queer, then you won't be allowed in.
  • No blatant racism or sexism.
  • Do not promote cryptocurrency. I'm not a fan of it. I have numorous reasons for this. If you have to ask for my reasons, you're probably not a good fit for this webring.
  • Sites created just to host NSFW. I don't want sites that just have adult content. That is not allowed.

Rules once you're in the webring

  • Keep up do date with the above rules. I may not let you know of rule changes. But generally, if you didn't break the rules previously, you probably won't now.
  • You must include the webring links on your site. Being part of a webring is one thing, participation is another thing. If you do not add the webring to your site, in a timely manner, I will remove you from the webring. I may give you notice before this, but I don't always; if you remove them a short time after I had asked to readd them, for one.
