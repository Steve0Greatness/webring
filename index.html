How to Join

Email me steve0greatnessiscool+wr [at] gmail.com with a link to your site, then I'll let you know your slug. Speaking of which, make sure to include something like the following on your site: 

<table>
    <tr>
        <td>
            <a
                href="https://steve0greatness.github.io/webring/sites/:slug:/prev.xhtml"
            >Prev</a>
        </td>
        <td>
            <a
                href="https://steve0greatness.github.io/webring"
            >
                <img
                    src="https://steve0greatness.github.io/webring/logo.webp"
                    width="100"
                    height="42"
                />
            </a>
        </td>
        <td>
            <a
                href="https://steve0greatness.github.io/webring/sites/:slug:/next.xhtml"
            >Next</a>
        </td>
    </tr>
</table>

Just make sure to replace :slug: with your slug.

Also, it doesn't have to be that specifically, that is just an example.
