How to Join

Email me steve0greatnessiscool+wr [at] gmail.com with a link to your site, then I'll will choose a slug, and tell you it. Speaking of which, make sure to include something like the following on your site once you have your slug:

        </td>
        <td>

        <td>
            <a
                href="https://steve0greatness.github.io/sites/:slug:/next.xhtml"

    </tr>

Just make sure to replace :slug: with your slug.

Also, it doesn't have to be that specifically, that is just an example.

What gets added to the webring?

I expect all sites to follow these rules:

  • No non-personal websites. All websites trying to join this webring must be a personal website. Whether your're hosting a blog, your artwork, or whatnot. It just has to be about you, not an organization, group, or a space of communication. You are allowed to have a guestbook, links to social media, etc, as long as it's not the primary focus of the site. Use your better judgment.
  • No slurs. If you use slurs on your website, I will not let you in the webring.
  • No anti-queer material. If you are against the rights or existance of those who are queer, then you won't be allowed in.
  • No racism or sexism.
  • Do not promote cryptocurrency. I'm not a fan of it. I have numorous reasons for this. If you have to ask for my reasons, you're probably not a good fit for this webring.
  • No sites created just to host NSFW. I don't want sites that just have adult content.

Rules once you're in the webring

  • Keep up do date with the above rules. I may not let you know of rule changes. But generally, if you didn't break the rules previously, you probably won't now.
  • You must include the webring links on your site. Being part of a webring is one thing, participation is another. If you do not add the webring to your site, in a timely manner, I will remove you from the webring. I may give you notice before this, but I don't always; if you remove them a short time after I had asked to readd them, for one.

Can I get removed from the webring?

Yes. If I see that you're violating the rules, you will be removed.
